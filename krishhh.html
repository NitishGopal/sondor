Our Story

Born from
a quiet Sunday.

SONDER started on a rainy Sunday in a Bangalore apartment. Meera had just quit her corporate job. Arjun had been sourcing coffee from small farms in Coorg for years, unable to find a home for it that felt right.

They sat together that morning with two mismatched mugs, a candle, and a stack of ceramics Meera had been making on weekends. And something clicked — not a business plan, but a feeling. That the best moments in life are the quiet ones. The unhurried ones.

SONDER is that feeling made tangible. Every product we carry is chosen for one reason: it makes ordinary moments feel extraordinary.

2019
The first bag of beans. Arjun drives to Coorg and brings back 20kg of washed Robusta. Sells it in handwritten brown paper bags to friends.
2021
The ceramics join. Meera starts wheel-throwing mugs in the kitchen. The first batch sells out in 3 hours on Instagram.
2023
SONDER launches. A proper store, a real team of 6, and a commitment to ethical sourcing across every product line.
2025
Today. 40,000 orders, 12 farm partners, carbon neutral shipping, and still the same two mismatched mugs on the kitchen shelf.
